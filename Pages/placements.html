Structured Placement Schemes

Learn about the different types of placement available to you and how a speculative application can boost your chances.

Many organisations offer a formalised placement scheme where students can apply for a specific role in an organisation.

Examples include:

  • Human Resources Placements
  • Environmental Health Placement
  • Safety Placement
  • Communications Placement
as well as more generalised roles, such as a Business operations or Management Trainee, where you can experience multiple business functions.

Typically these types of roles are advertised on the University vacancy system), external websites such as RateMyPlacement as well as on the organisationâ€™s own website.

Top Tip!


If you are interested in a particular organisation then apply early! Application deadlines are sometimes brought forward when the organisation has received a sufficient number of high quality applications.
