How To Stand Out

One tip should be remembered above all others!

Top Tip!


Whether you're applying online, via CV or in person:
Tailor your application to the job specification!

In more detail:

Do Do Not
Give the employer the information they are asking for Mention a particular skill they have without giving an example of how they have gained that skill
Address each point on the essential criteria with specific examples from their experiences
