How To Stand Out

Learn how to make your application the best it can possibly be.

One tip should be remembered above all others!

Top Tip!


Whether you're applying online, via CV or in person:
Tailor your application to the job specification!

In more detail:

Do Do Not
  • Give the employer the information they are asking for
  • Address each point on the essential criteria with specific examples from your experiences
  • Mention a particular skill you have without giving an example of how you gained that skill

Preparation, research and thinking about what they can offer are three things which will help you to succeed in the application process.

Employers want to know that you have the skills and ability to do the job, the motivation to do the job and a desire to work for their organisation.
