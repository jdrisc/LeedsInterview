Speculative Applications

Learn how speculative applications can improve your chances and lead to unique placement opportunities.

If a sector or organisation doesn't advertise formal placements, then you might need to be a bit more creative when searching for opportunities in these areas.

Top Tip!


Start by compiling a list of organisations which you are interested in and contact each to see if they would consider you a placement student.

There are lots of different ways to approach organisations, including:

  • telephone calls (great for a quick answer)
  • sending a CV and covering letter
  • in-person
  • through LinkedIn or via your own networks (e.g. identifying possible organisations through friends and family contacts).

You should consider more than your existing personal networks. Peers on courses and staff within schools may also have contacts within their networks which may be of use. The Leeds Network is a great way to contact University of Leeds alumni who might be able to give advice too.
